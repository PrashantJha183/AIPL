What services does Agratas Infotech® provide?
+

We offer a comprehensive range of design services, including branding, UI/UX design, and development. Our skilled team of designers and developers collaborate to create cohesive and impactful solutions tailored to your unique needs and goals.

How long does a typical project take?
+

The timeline for each project depends on its complexity and scope. Once we've discussed your project requirements and objectives, we'll provide you with a detailed timeline and keep you updated throughout the process. We strive to deliver high-quality work within the agreed-upon timeframe.

How much does it cost to work with Agratas Infotech®?
+

We understand that every project is unique, so we provide customized quotes based on your specific needs and goals. Generally, we operate on an hourly basis with a rate of $65 per hour. We typically work on projects with a minimum budget of $1,000. To get a better understanding of your project's cost, please reach out to us and provide some details about your requirements. We'll be happy to provide you with a tailored quote that aligns with your project's scope and objectives.
